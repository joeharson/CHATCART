How to Use the Mall Chatbot:

  • 1. Search Products: Type or ask for a product to see available stores and offers.
  • 2. Find Stores: Ask for a store’s location, opening hours, or directions.
  • 3. View Discounts: Explore current promotions across different categories.
  • 4. Get Directions: Ask for directions to any store or facility within the mall.
  • 5. Inquire About Services: Find information on mall services like parking, dining, or amenities.

How Not to Use the Mall Chatbot:

  • 1. Avoid Vague Questions: Don’t ask general queries like “I need something” – be specific.
  • 2. Don’t Spam Repeated Queries: Asking the same question multiple times won’t speed up responses.
  • 3. Avoid Irrelevant Requests: The chatbot cannot help with non-mall-related inquiries.
  • 4. Don’t Use Slang or Abbreviations: Stick to clear and simple language for better results.
  • 5. No Sensitive Information: Never share personal or payment details through the chatbot.
